About Me

The story behind the shutter

Photography lets me hold on to feelings that disappear in a fraction of a second. Whether I'm covering opening night, a quiet rehearsal, or an afternoon that felt like nothing until it became everything, I'm looking for the moments hiding inside ordinary ones.

My work moves between the stage and the everyday — from the choreography of a musical number to the stillness of a picnic blanket in late light.

Based In Vermillion, South Dakota — available worldwide
